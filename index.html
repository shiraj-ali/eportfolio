What we do

Dependence on technology makes the care and maintenance of your information systems a crucial business operation. The upkeep of these information systems is often overlooked as an ongoing business investment, yet an efficient and stable IT system is vital in a competitive marketplace and failure to maintain technology levels or adequate back up or Disaster recovery can prove very costly. En Route Solutions can take away the worries of managing your systems with their Smart Support range of IT support services combining speed, expertise and commitment.

Smart Management

“Smart Support” Management Pack

Smart Support Core

This covers your business in a day to day support function, supporting any issues that arise around the daily functioning of your environment. This does not cover any additions, moves or changes to your environment.

Smart Support Extended

Selecting this option covers your Information Technology department completely for all eventualities under reasonable use except for project work which would be quoted at discounted rates.

Business Benefits

With expertise across the entire spectrum of specialist business technologies, En Route Solutions offers IT Support Services to maintain your systems at peak performance levels over the long term. If problems do arise, we will manage them to minimize the disruption to your organization. With En Route’s own Disaster Recovery solutions your downtime is always at a minimum.

Cost Benefits

From our experience we have found that if you are already using an external support company that we can offer savings and more often than not a superior and more comprehensive service for your IT support needs, and if you are currently using internal IT support resources we can show a better service and significantly lower costs and without the need for holiday cover etc.

Peace of Mind

Peace of mind is one of those intangible benefits that are difficult to put a price on, but you know it’s valuable. When you sign up for our “Smart Support” managed service, or take advantage of our Disaster Recovery program or one of our many hosted services, Peace of Mind comes naturally, since your company will enjoy the benefits of stability, consistency, and seamless interoperability.
